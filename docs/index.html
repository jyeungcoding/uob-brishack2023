Yoga Companion

Score: 50

Introduction

Yoga Companion is a groundbreaking new technology utilising TensorFlow.js (#WebML) to improve our mental and physical health by giving anyone with an internet connection access to a yoga coaching system.

Instructions

Simply choose a demo video using the leftmost button in the header and a second screen will appear shortly on the right. Please try refreshing the page if you do not see the second screen appear.

Tracking lines will be mapped onto your body: these are green when your pose is correct and red when you are out of position.

Additionally, a Score tracks your overall position: you can save and view Scores with the Score tab in the header.
